Pharmacokinetic Equations

Elimination rate constant: ke=CLVd

Half-life: t1/2=0.693ke

Initial concentration: Cp=DVd

Single dose plasma: Cp=Cp0·e−ke·t

Oral single dose: Cp=F·D·kaVd(ka−ke)·(e−ke·t−e−ka·t)

Steady state: Css=F·DCL·τ

Clearance: CL=Dose·FAUC

Trough multiple dose: Cmin=Cp0·e−ke·τ1−e−ke·τ
